KDE4 在 FreeBSD 安裝好後須如何執行 ?

Jan 8th, 2010

For KDE3:

% echo “exec startkde” > ~/.xinitrc

For KDE4:

% echo “exec /usr/local/kde4/bin/startkde” > ~/.xinitrc

Now, whenever the X Window System is invoked with startx, KDE will be the desktop.
