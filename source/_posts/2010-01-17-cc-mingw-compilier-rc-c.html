--- layout: post title: "C/C++ MinGW 中,如何 Compilier *.rc 和 *.c" date: 2010-01-08T14:48:00+08:00 categories: - C - Compiler - MinGW ---
ps: *.rc is resource file
1. 先用 *.rc 產生 *.o 檔案.
2. 在 Complilier C/C++時,將 *.o 加入編譯中.

Ex:

windres resources.rc resources.o
gcc -o main.exe main.c resources.o
